Black Coffee

Description

This is how you make a cup of strong black coffee. The ingredients are pretty simple.

Ingredients

  • 250g Water
  • 50g Coffee (ground)
  • Coffee filter
  • Cup

Steps

  1. Heat up water to a rolling boil
  2. Place coffee filter into cup
  3. Place coffee grounds into coffee filter
  4. Pour boiling water directly over coffee grounds
  5. Let steep for 2 minutes
  6. Lift coffee filter, allowing extracted coffee to drain into cup
  7. Squeeze out excess liquid from coffee grounds in coffee filter
